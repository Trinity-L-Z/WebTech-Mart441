What does Trinity love to do?

The real question we should be asking is, what DON'T I love? I am a true explorer, always evolving and daring to test new ideas. Primarily, I have always loved reading, video games, and cinema. I love to paint and draw--it runs in my family!--and am currently endevouring to sell beaded jewelry. But don't be fooled by my cozy hobbies! I am an outdoor enthusiast, a lover of bouldering and yoga, and live for swimming in the summer.

Inspiration & Ambitions

The majority of my inspiration comes from the natural world. I am also the first in my family to pursue a bachelor's degree, let alone one in art! It was my grandma's dream to be an animator, and my mom's to be an artist. Now, here I am with one semester left in the Media Arts program.

I call myself a storyteller. I believe the stories I have to tell are important, and this urge has driven me to pick up various skills in order to properly express myself. The dream is to work on animated movies and TV shows in some capacity with a team of people who share the same values as I do. But things like that take time!
