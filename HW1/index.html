What does Trinity love to do?

The real question we should be asking is, what DON'T I love? I am a true explorer, always evolving and daring to test new ideas. Primarily, I have always loved reading, video games, and cinema. I love to paint and draw--it runs in my family!--and am currently endevouring to sell beaded jewelry. But don't be fooled by my cozy hobbies! I am an outdoor enthusiast, a lover of bouldering and yoga, and live for swimming in the summer.
